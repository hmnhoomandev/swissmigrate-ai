# Social integration in Zurich for workers
Timeframe: Days 30-365
Priority: 9

In Zurich, work can help you build routine, but integration also needs language practice and local contacts outside the workplace. Start with one recurring activity that fits your schedule.

## Actions
- Ask your municipality about local integration offers and German conversation groups.
- Join one regular activity after work, such as a language cafe, library event, sports group, or volunteer activity.
- Keep contacts for the Zurich integration office, your commune, and any trusted community advisor.

## Services
- [Kantonale Fachstelle Integration Zurich](https://www.zh.ch/de/migration-integration/integration.html) - Cantonal integration information.
- [AOZ](https://www.aoz.ch) - Integration and social support programs in Zurich.
