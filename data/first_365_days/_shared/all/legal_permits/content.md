# Legal permits
Timeframe: Days 1-30
Priority: 3

Your permit controls many practical rights: residence, work, travel, study, family reunification, and deadlines. Use this guide as practical orientation only and verify legal decisions with the canton or a qualified legal advisor.

## Actions
- Identify your permit or pending procedure and write down renewal or appeal deadlines.
- Check whether work, travel, study, and address changes require permission.
- Ask for qualified legal help before missing a deadline or submitting an appeal.
