# Language learning
Timeframe: Days 15-120
Priority: 6

Language courses are often the fastest way to understand letters, appointments, housing, work, and daily life. Subsidized or integration-linked courses vary by canton and migration status.

## Actions
- Ask the integration office or social worker about subsidized courses.
- Choose a realistic weekly schedule and combine classes with daily practice.
- Keep certificates because they may help with permits, work, or study.
