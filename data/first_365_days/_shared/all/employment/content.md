# Employment
Timeframe: Days 30-180
Priority: 4

Work rights and job search steps depend on your permit, profession, canton, and whether your qualifications need recognition. Before accepting work, confirm that your permit allows it and that salary and contract terms are clear.

## Actions
- Check whether your current permit allows work and whether your employer must notify authorities.
- Prepare a Swiss-style CV and collect certificates, references, and diplomas.
- Ask about recognition of foreign qualifications if your profession is regulated.
