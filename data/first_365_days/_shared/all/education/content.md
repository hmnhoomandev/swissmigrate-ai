# Education
Timeframe: Days 30-180
Priority: 5

School, vocational training, university access, and bridging programs depend on age, previous education, language level, canton, and status. Start by collecting certificates and asking the local education office what pathway fits.

## Actions
- Gather diplomas, transcripts, language certificates, and translations if available.
- Ask the school, municipality, or canton education office about placement and bridging options.
- Check deadlines early for vocational training, university, or continuing education.
