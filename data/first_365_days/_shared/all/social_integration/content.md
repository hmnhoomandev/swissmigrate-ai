# Social integration
Timeframe: Days 30-365
Priority: 9

Building a local network makes daily life easier: language practice, trusted contacts, community events, and help understanding systems. Start with low-pressure places such as libraries, language cafes, sports groups, or neighborhood centers.

## Actions
- Ask your municipality or integration office about local community activities.
- Join one recurring activity where you can practice the local language.
- Save contacts for trusted helpers and official offices.
