# Financial support
Timeframe: Days 30-180
Priority: 7

Financial support, taxes, health insurance subsidies, and social assistance vary strongly by canton and migration status. Keep all letters about money and ask before ignoring payment deadlines.

## Actions
- Ask whether you are eligible for social assistance, health insurance subsidies, or student/work-related support.
- Keep a simple monthly budget and save copies of all official payment letters.
- Ask before signing loans or contracts you do not understand.
