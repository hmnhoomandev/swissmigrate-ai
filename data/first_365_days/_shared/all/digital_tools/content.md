# Digital tools
Timeframe: Days 1-30
Priority: 13

The right apps can make transport, weather, translation, appointments, and document handling easier. Use translation tools for first understanding, but confirm important legal or medical information with a human or official source.

## Actions
- Install SBB Mobile for public transport and MeteoSwiss for weather alerts.
- Use a calendar with reminders for appointments and deadlines.
- Store scanned copies of important documents securely.

## Services
- [SBB Mobile](https://www.sbb.ch/en/tickets-offers/tickets/mobile-tickets/sbb-mobile.html) - Public transport app.
- [MeteoSwiss](https://www.meteoswiss.admin.ch/) - Official Swiss weather information.

## Sources
- [SBB](https://www.sbb.ch/en) - Public transport information.
- [MeteoSwiss](https://www.meteoswiss.admin.ch/) - Official weather information.
