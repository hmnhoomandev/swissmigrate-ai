# Healthcare & insurance basics
Timeframe: Days 1-90
Priority: 1

Health insurance and access to care depend on your permit, canton, and income situation. Confirm your exact obligation early, keep all letters from insurers and authorities, and ask a trusted office before missing a deadline.

## Actions
- Check whether you must choose a Swiss health insurer or whether your support program handles coverage.
- Save emergency numbers: 144 for medical emergencies, 117 for police, and 118 for fire.
- Ask a doctor, social service, or integration office how to find basic medical care in your area.
