# Registration/admin
Timeframe: Days 1-14
Priority: 11

Registration, address updates, appointments, and official letters are the base of many other steps. Rules differ by canton, municipality, and status, so confirm where you must register and which documents are needed.

## Actions
- Confirm your registration office and appointment requirements.
- Keep copies of confirmations, permits, address documents, and appointment letters.
- Tell the relevant office quickly if your address changes.
