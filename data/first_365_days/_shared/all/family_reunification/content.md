# Family reunification
Timeframe: When relevant
Priority: 12

Family reunification rules are legal and status-specific. Deadlines, income, housing, identity documents, and proof of family relationships can matter. Get qualified advice before submitting or missing anything.

## Actions
- Check eligibility with the canton, migration authority, or a qualified legal advisor.
- Collect identity, marriage, birth, custody, and residence documents early.
- Track deadlines and keep proof of every submission.
