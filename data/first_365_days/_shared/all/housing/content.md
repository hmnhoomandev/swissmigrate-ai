# Housing basics
Timeframe: Days 1-120
Priority: 2

Housing rules, deposits, social support, and access to subsidized help vary by canton and migration status. Do not sign unclear contracts quickly; keep copies of applications, rent offers, deposit payments, and official housing letters.

## Actions
- Confirm whether your current status gives you assigned housing, private housing options, or student/work-related housing.
- Ask about deposit rules, notice periods, and whether rent support is possible.
- Keep every rental document and payment confirmation.
