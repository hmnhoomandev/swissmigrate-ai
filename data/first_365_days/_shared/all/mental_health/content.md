# Mental health
Timeframe: Any time
Priority: 10

Stress, isolation, trauma, and uncertainty are common during migration. Asking for help is normal. If you are in immediate danger or cannot stay safe, use emergency services.

## Actions
- Call 143 for urgent emotional support if you need someone to talk to.
- Ask a doctor, social worker, or trusted office for mental health referral options.
- Keep a simple routine: sleep, food, movement, appointments, and one social contact.

## Services
- [Die Dargebotene Hand 143](https://www.143.ch/) - Confidential emotional support.
