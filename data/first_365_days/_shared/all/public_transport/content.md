# Public transport
Timeframe: Days 1-60
Priority: 8

Public transport can become much cheaper with the right pass, app, or local subscription. Compare daily travel needs before buying a yearly product.

## Actions
- Install SBB Mobile and save your regular routes.
- Compare single tickets, local monthly passes, SwissPass, and Half Fare Travelcard.
- Ask whether your school, employer, municipality, or support program offers transport help.

## Services
- [SBB](https://www.sbb.ch/en) - Swiss train and public transport information.
