# Housing in Geneva for students
Timeframe: Days 1-120
Priority: 2

Student housing in Geneva is often limited, so apply early and keep documents ready. Your university, school, or training institution may have specific housing leads that are more relevant than the general rental market.

## Actions
- Ask your university or school about student residences and verified housing boards.
- Prepare admission confirmation, ID, permit information, proof of funds, and guarantor documents if requested.
- Avoid sending money before the housing offer is verified.

## Services
- [University of Geneva housing](https://www.unige.ch/batiment/service-batiments/campus/housing/) - Student housing information.
- [Canton Geneva housing portal](https://www.ge.ch/logement) - Official Geneva housing information.
