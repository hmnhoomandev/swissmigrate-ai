# Housing in Geneva for workers
Timeframe: Days 1-120
Priority: 2

In Geneva, housing can be competitive and expensive. As a worker, focus on stable proof of income, clear rental documents, and checking whether your municipality or employer can point you to practical housing support.

## Actions
- Prepare payslips, work contract, permit or permit application proof, ID, and references before applying.
- Ask before paying deposits or reservation fees, especially if the offer is not from a trusted agency or landlord.
- Check whether your commune has local housing information or social support if your rent becomes unmanageable.

## Services
- [Canton Geneva housing portal](https://www.ge.ch/logement) - Official Geneva housing information.
- [Hospice general](https://www.hospicegeneral.ch) - Social support in Geneva when eligible.
